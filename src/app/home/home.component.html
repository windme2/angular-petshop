Pet Care Tips

Exercise for Dogs

Keep your dog active with daily walks or playtime. Aim for at least 30 minutes to keep them healthy and happy!

Grooming for Cats

Brush your cat weekly to reduce shedding and prevent hairballs. It’s also a great bonding time!

Balanced Nutrition

Feed your pets high-quality food suited to their age and breed. Consult a vet for the best diet plan.
